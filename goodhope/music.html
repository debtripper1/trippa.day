01. ALL MY FRIENDS ARE DEAD AND DYING
LP — 2026
"Now I'm mature I see a ticking timebomb"

Eight songs about friendship, grief, and the people we outlive. Recorded in a bedroom that doubled as a storage unit. The title is not hyperbolic.
"If I had to watch you die, I'd rather kill myself"
02. A LITTLE SPIRAL OF MY OWN
LP — 2026
"I spent my early twenties smoking, snorting, suffocating"

Seven songs about addiction, performance, and the slow erasure of self. Written in bars, hotel rooms, and the back of a car somewhere between New Orleans and San Francisco.
"I don't know if i'm alive, if anything's real anymore"
03. EVERYTHING WILL BE OKAY, IN THE END
LP — 2026
"I don't want to be, anymore"

Eight songs about the possibility of an ending. Contains the interlude "No Words" — exactly two lines long. Closes with "Bloom," the first song in the catalogue that sounds like it might want to continue living. The title is not a guarantee. It is a question.
"the Choir Invisible singing, calling me out to join in"
