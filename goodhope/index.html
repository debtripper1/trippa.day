good hope
the good hope project — melbourne, australia

Three records in one year. A single the year before. Recorded in bedrooms, garages, basements, and one hotel room in New Orleans.

The music exists somewhere between folk and noise, between acoustic confession and digital decay. Songs are built from loops, field recordings, late-night voice memos, and whatever instrument was within arm's reach.
